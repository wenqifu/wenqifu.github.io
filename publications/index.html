  2. GradES: Significantly Faster Training in Transformers with Gradient-Based Early Stopping
    Qifu Wen, Xi Zeng, Zihan Zhou, and 4 more authors
    2025
    Preprint; under review at AAAI 2027
    Abs arXiv Bib

    GradES is a gradient-based early stopping algorithm that individually freezes transformer components when their gradients fall below a convergence threshold. Achieves 1.70–1.77× faster full-parameter fine-tuning at neutral accuracy on Qwen3-0.6B, Llama-3.1-8B, and Qwen3-14B.
